How to Style a Quiff

To style a quiff, begin with clean hair and select the appropriate styling products such as pomade or wax. After washing and towel-drying your hair, apply the chosen product evenly throughout. Utilize a blow dryer to shape the quiff, directing the front section of your hair upwards and slightly back for volume and height. Keep the sides neat and tidy using a comb or your fingers. Once styled, finish with a light mist of hairspray to set the quiff in place. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't hesitate to experiment with different techniques until you achieve the desired look that complements your individual style.

AW24 Menswear Trends

When it comes to men's fashion, the AW24 season brought a range of exciting trends to the forefront. From the runways to the streets, we're seeing a fusion of classic tailoring with contemporary twists. One standout trend is the resurgence of heritage patterns like houndstooth and plaid, adding a touch of timeless elegance to any ensemble. Oversized silhouettes also made a bold statement, with exaggerated proportions and relaxed fits dominating the scene. Another key trend is the embrace of rich, earthy tones like forest green, burnt orange, and deep burgundy, bringing warmth and depth to winter wardrobes. And let's not forget about the revival of retro-inspired pieces, from shearling jackets to chunky knitwear, offering a nod to vintage aesthetics with a modern sensibility. At the intersection of style and comfort, AW24 menswear trends offer endless opportunities for self-expression and sartorial experimentation
